How TripSync Hub Flows

01

Set Your Route

Enter where you are and where you need to go once. TripSync maps nearby stations and bus stands, then builds a clean list of options.

02

Blend Train & Bus

Mix trains for the long stretch and buses for the last mile. We treat every leg as part of a single journey, not a separate booking.

03

Sync Food to Time

Pick partner kitchens on your path. Pre‑order so your meals appear at boarding or during a pause, without adding mental load.
