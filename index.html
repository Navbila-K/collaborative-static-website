The Flow

How TripSync Flows

Three elegantly sequenced steps bring your journey from intent to experience — without the usual fragmentation.

01

Set Your Route

Enter your origin and destination once. TripSync maps nearby stations and bus stands, then presents a clean, ranked list of options — sorted by what matters to you.

02

Blend Train & Bus

Mix trains for the long stretch and buses for the last mile. Every leg is treated as part of a single journey — one booking surface, no separate tabs.

03

Sync Food to Time

Select partner kitchens along your path. Pre-order so meals appear at boarding or during a pause — timed so precisely it removes a decision, not adds one.
