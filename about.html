The Journey Blueprint

TripSync Hub is our thought experiment: what if planning a train or bus trip — and the food that follows you along the way — felt as carefully designed as an airport lounge?

Instead of splitting the traveler’s attention across multiple apps, counters, and paper tickets, we imagine a single surface that understands routes, seats, and meals as one continuous story. Every glow, gradient, and card layout on this site is a prototype panel from that imagined control room.

Technically, the project is intentionally minimal: static HTML, CSS, and a thin layer of JavaScript for motion — proving that a premium, cinematic travel interface does not require heavy frameworks. The focus stays on hierarchy, timing, and clarity so real travelers could move through decisions quickly, even under pressure.
